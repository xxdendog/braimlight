DEVELOPING A DESIGN PROJECT WE USE THE PRINCIPLES OF MULTILAYER LIGHTING

Based on our knowledge, experience and an integrated approach, we will transform your space using only one tool - light!

01 — 03

GENERAL LIGHTING

The goal is to provide a sufficient amount of light in the room, to display the general concept of the room design. Will become a kind of backdrop for everything else (furniture, decor, textiles, etc.).

  • Pendant lights
  • Track and center lights
  • Duplex ceilings
02 — 03

ACCENT LIGHT

These are techniques for illuminating objects or surfaces in order to draw attention to these objects. The light emphasizes the texture of the surface, objects, architectural features of the room and gives additional soft illumination.

  • Illumination of niches, cabinets, mirrors
  • Sconces, floor lamps, table lamps
  • Spotlights
03 — 03

DECORATIVE LIGHT

Light architecture emphasizes the conceptual focus of the space and can tell a lot about the owners. Some styles are impossible to create without proper lighting.

  • Light scenes and installations
  • Light lines, profile systems
  • Decor
